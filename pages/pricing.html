FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to be technical to use ZwiskyBot?

Not at all. We handle everything — building, hosting, and connecting the bot to your WhatsApp number. You just tell us what you need.

How do customers reach my bot?

They message your business WhatsApp number like normal. The bot responds instantly. You can still take over the conversation manually whenever you want.

Does it support M-Pesa?

Yes. The bot can send M-Pesa payment instructions, confirm when payment is received, and send automated receipts to customers.

How do I pay for ZwiskyBot?

We accept M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, and bank transfer. No international card needed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time. No contracts, no penalties.
