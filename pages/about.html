Our Story

Why we started.

Most Tanzanian businesses are still managing their WhatsApp manually — replying to the same questions over and over, missing messages, losing orders at 11pm when they're asleep.

ZwiskyBot was built to fix that. We take the repetitive work off your hands and put it on a bot that never sleeps, never gets tired, and never misses a message.

We're based in Arusha and we build bots that understand the Tanzanian market — M-Pesa, Swahili, local pricing, local businesses.
